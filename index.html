I was born and raised on Oahu, Hawaii. I spent almost a decade living in various states on the mainland, but I still consider Hawaii my home. I traveled 1-2 times a year either with family or a school group from 5th grade through high school. After that, I started venturing out on my own and still have a long list of places I'd love to visit.

I love creating order in the midst of chaos. This could be reorganizing a shelf, doing a massive overhaul of a long-neglected basement, or creating a functional a fun room for a burgeoning teenager. Most recently I've taken up woodworking as a way to take unfinished wood and refine it into functional household pieces.

Fun fact: I studied Hawaiian language in high school and a little into college. No, I am not fluent, but I can generally understand the content of written or spoken Hawaiian.
